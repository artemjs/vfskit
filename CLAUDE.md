# vfskit

Universal abstraction over any VFS (memory, disk, S3, ...). One `VFS` interface,
composable adapters + middleware + a remote bridge. Ships as `vfskit` (npm/node) and
`vfskit-front` (jsDelivr/browser) under one brand.

## Rules

- No comments in code.
- No watermarks in commits (no `Co-Authored-By`, no generated-by trailers).
- Minimize lines, characters, and downloaded bytes. Compact is the priority.
- TypeScript strict, ESM, English only.
